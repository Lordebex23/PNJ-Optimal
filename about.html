Who are we

GLOBAL REACH, LOCAL EXPERTISE

PNJ Optimal Technology Limited is a 100% indigenous company established in 2017 by the founder Mr. Samuel Chukwu. Mr. Samuel Chukwu is a qualified Electronic Communications Engineer and holds extensive engineering experience within the Embedded System project development, Industrial Automation, Consumers Electronics & Non-destructive Testing (NDT) industries. PNJ Optimal will provide Consultancy, Schematic, PCB Layout and Gerber exporting services to its clientele to best position them to manage and improve their product and technical efficiencies. The consultancy will provide quality system management within their technical division inclusive of process mapping, equipment setup, drafting reports and work instructions, training and evaluation of technical staff in accordance to a national standard, company quality manual and (or) manufacturers guidelines. In addition, PNJ Optimal can provide services that will improve the image and brand of its clients by providing effective services, conduct and managing research and development as well as repair and calibration services by professionally qualified personnel. Although PNJ Optimal a Small-Scale Company in the initial stages of the operation, the long-term goal is to establish a solid brand which will be trusted within the industry. The organization seeks to grow not only its profit share but also its market share within Nigeria and the continent. PNJ Optimal believes that its growth aspirations will be met while taking strides to grow its footprint on the continent.
