Our Services

At PNJ Optimal, we pride ourselves on delivering cutting-edge electronic and automation solutions tailored to meet the demands of individuals, businesses, and industries. With a focus on precision, innovation, and reliability, our range of services is designed to solve real-world challenges efficiently.

What we do

Calibration and Verification of Equipments

Accuracy is non-negotiable in today's fast-paced industries. We provide comprehensive calibration and verification services for a wide range of electronic and industrial instruments. Our process ensures that your equipment adheres to strict industry standards, maintaining reliability and performance.

Re-Engineering of Obsolete Machines

Stayahead in your operations by modernizing your equipment. Our re-engineering services transform outdated machines into efficient systems by integrating the latest technologies. This cost-effective solution is ideal for prolonging the lifespan of critical assests.

Electronics Repair and Trouble Shooting

We diagnose and repair faulty electronic devices, from consumer electronics to industrial-grade machinery. Our expert team ensures quick turn around times and restores your devices to optimal working conditions.

Customized Embedded System Development

We specialize in the design and development of embedded systems tailored to your unique requirements. Whether for IoT applications, industrial automation, or specialized projects, our solutions deliver reliability, efficiency, and scalability.

Firmware and Microcontroller Development

From firmware development to microcontroller programming, we provide tailored software solutions for embedded systems and automation projects.

Preventive Maintenance Servicing

Don't wait for breakdowns to disrupt your workflow. Our preventive maintenance services help identify potential issues before they occur, ensuring uninterrupted performance and extending the life of your equipment.

PCB Design and Prototyping

We offer professional PCB (Printed Circuit Board) design and prototyping services to support your product development. From concept to production, we ensure high-quality and precision-driven solutions.

PLC Automation

Optimize your industrial processes with our PLC automation solutions. From design and programming to installation and maintenance, we help streamline operations, reduce costs, and improve productivity.

Automation Consultancy

Need help optimizing your operations? Our automation consultancy services offer expert guidance on implementing effective solutions to improve efficiency and productivity.
